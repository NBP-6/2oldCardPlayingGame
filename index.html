How to Play — a fan project inspired by Sea of Thieves
The Goal

Sink the enemy ship before yours goes under. Your ship starts at 15 HP. Reach 0 and you lose the battle.

Your Two Fighters
  • Your Ship — the main target. Protect it at all costs.
  • Your Pirate — attacks, heals, and causes chaos. If they die, they're sent to the Ferry of the Damned and you skip your next turn before they respawn.
How a Turn Works

Each turn you get 3 mana — spend it or lose it, it doesn't carry over. You draw up to a hand of 5 cards at the start of each turn. Play as many cards as your mana allows, then end your turn. When your deck runs out, your discard pile reshuffles and you draw again.

Card Types
  • 🔵 Naval — damage the enemy ship directly
  • 🔴 Pirate — attack the enemy pirate
  • 🟢 Repair — restore HP to your ship
  • 🟩 Healing — restore HP to your pirate
  • 🟡 Chaos — disrupt, draw, buff, or gamble

Cards marked Consumed vanish after use instead of going to discard. Powerful, but you only get them once per deck cycle.

The Streak

Win a battle and your streak grows. Lose and it resets to zero. The goal is to reach streak 10. That's when you become a Pirate Legend.

Battle Modifiers

Each battle after the first introduces a new rule — storms that damage both ships, a megalodon that strikes randomly, TNT kegs appearing in your hand, the Kraken blocking your cannons. The further you go, the more chaotic it gets. The rule stays visible during the fight so you never forget it.

Rewards

Win a battle and choose one reward — either a new card added to your deck, or a permanent run bonus. Rewards come in three tiers: Common, Rare (from battle 4), and Legendary (from battle 7). You can only pick one Legendary per run.

The Final Tide — Battle 10

The Red Sea closes in. Every few turns, zone damage hits both ships and escalates. If the battle isn't finished when the zone fully closes, the ship with the least HP is sunk. No stalling. Finish it.
